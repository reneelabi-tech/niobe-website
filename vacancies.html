Careers

Join our
team

We are always looking for skilled, attentive therapists and beauty professionals to join Niobe Spa across our five locations in Accra and Tema.

Niobe Spa is an award-winning spa and salon with over twenty years of experience. We hold our team to a high standard, and we invest in them accordingly. If you are a qualified therapist, nail technician, beauty professional or salon manager who takes their craft seriously, we would like to hear from you. To apply for any current or future role, please send your CV and a short covering message to us via WhatsApp on 0561 11 00 00 or use the contact form on our contact page.

Spa Therapist

Multiple locations

We are looking for qualified massage and body therapists with experience using professional skincare brands. Elemis training is an advantage. Full-time and part-time positions available across East Legon, Cantonments and Tema.

Nail Technician

East Legon · Community 18

Beauty Therapist

All locations

Lash extensions, brow treatments, waxing and threading. Precision and client care are non-negotiable. Experience with semi-permanent lash systems is preferred.

Accra locations

Client-facing role managing bookings, guest arrival and in-spa communication. Attention to detail, warmth and composure under pressure. Experience in a premium spa or hospitality environment preferred.
