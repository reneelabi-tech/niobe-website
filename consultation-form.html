Treatment Specific

Facial treatment questions

Facial treatments involve applying products directly to your skin. These questions help us identify contraindications and choose the safest products and techniques for your skin type and current condition.
  • 1. Do you have any active skin infections, cold sores, or open wounds on your face or neck?
  • 2. Do you have a skin condition (eczema, rosacea, psoriasis, severe active acne)?
  • 3. Are you currently using Retinol, Retinoids, or have you used Accutane in the last 6 months?
  • 4. Have you had a chemical peel, laser, microdermabrasion, or dermaplaning in the last 2 weeks?
  • 5. Have you had facial waxing, threading, or electrolysis in the last 48 hours?
  • 6. Do you have sunburn, windburn, or very sensitive skin right now?
  • 7. Are you using any prescription topical medications on your face?
  • 8. Do you have any known allergies to skincare products, fragrances, or essential oils?
  • 9. Have you ever had an allergic reaction to a facial product or treatment?
  • 10. Do you have dental implants, permanent fillers, or recent cosmetic injectables (Botox, fillers)?

Treatment Specific

Steam & sauna questions

Steam and sauna sessions raise your core body temperature significantly. Certain health conditions can make this unsafe. Your answers allow us to ensure your session is conducted safely and within appropriate time limits.
  • 1. Do you have a heart condition, chest pain, or history of heart attack or stroke?
  • 2. Do you have high or low blood pressure (hypertension / hypotension)?
  • 3. Do you have asthma, COPD, or any respiratory/breathing condition?
  • 4. Do you have diabetes or any condition affecting blood sugar regulation?
  • 5. Do you have epilepsy, seizures, or a neurological condition?
  • 6. Are you feeling unwell, feverish, or do you have an active infection today?
  • 7. Do you have any skin conditions worsened by heat (rosacea, severe eczema)?
  • 8. Are you taking any medications that affect heat tolerance or cause photosensitivity?
  • 9. Have you consumed alcohol within the last 4 hours?
  • 10. Have you had surgery or a medical procedure within the last 4 weeks?
  • 11. Do you have any implanted devices (pacemaker, metal implants)?
