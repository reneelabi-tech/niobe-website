options

We accept a range of payment methods in person and remotely. A deposit may be required to confirm your appointment.

In person

Cash & Card

We accept cash and Visa card payments at the counter at any of our five locations. Please bring the correct payment — we cannot always make change for very large notes.

Deposit policy

A 100% non-refundable deposit is required to confirm bookings for facials, massages, spa packages and body wraps. Deposits are applied in full to your service on the day. Because deposits are non-refundable, we are unable to offer refunds for cancellations — please contact us as early as possible if you need to reschedule.
