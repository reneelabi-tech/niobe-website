Niobe Beauty has been one of Ghana's leading spa and salon destinations for over two decades. Across five locations in Accra and Tema, we offer a consistently high standard of treatment, technique and attention.

We work with Elemis — one of the world's most respected professional skincare brands — alongside a full menu of body treatments, hammam ceremonies, beauty services and thermal facilities. Every session is tailored to the individual.
