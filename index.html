Niobe Spa is an upscale salon and spa with five locations across Greater Accra and Tema. Since opening, we have built a reputation for precise, attentive treatments delivered by trained therapists using Elemis, Murad and other professional-grade products. Whether you are here for a thirty-minute facial or a full day of care, the experience is the same: considered service, clean spaces and results you can see and feel.

Winner of the World Luxury Spa Award, Niobe is where beauty, wellness and personal care are taken seriously.
