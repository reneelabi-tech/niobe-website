Body Massage

Our massage menu includes Elemis full-body treatments, targeted therapies and specialist techniques. Every session is tailored by your therapist, who will adjust pressure, focus areas and pace based on what your body needs. Available in 30, 60 and 90-minute formats at most locations.

Available at All five locations

To help us tailor your massage safely and effectively, you may be asked to complete a short consultation form before your appointment. This allows your therapist to understand your pressure preferences, any health considerations and areas of focus. Please inform us of any medical conditions, recent injuries or pregnancy at the time of booking.

Body Massage

All massage treatments are carried out by trained therapists and use high-quality Elemis products. Your therapist will discuss your pressure preferences and any areas of concern before the session begins. Pregnancy massage is available from the second trimester onward.

Elemis Deeper Than Deep Hot Stone Massage

60 / 90 min GHS 370 / 480 Book Now

Smooth, heated stones are placed along the body and used to massage deep into the muscles, releasing tension that hands alone sometimes cannot reach. Warmed oils are applied throughout, hydrating the skin while the sustained heat softens tight areas across the back, shoulders and legs.

Available at all five locations

Elemis Freestyle Deep Tissue Massage

60 / 90 min GHS 360 / 460 Book Now

A firm, focused massage tailored entirely to your body. Your therapist works into the areas carrying the most tension, using deep pressure to ease knotted muscle and improve mobility. Suited to clients who carry physical strain from exercise, posture or prolonged sitting.

Available at all five locations

Elemis Garden of England Rose Restore Massage

60 / 90 min GHS 340 / 440 Book Now

A full-body Swedish massage using rose-infused oils. Long, flowing strokes ease tension and improve circulation. Lighter pressure than deep tissue, with a calming pace and warm floral fragrance. A good choice when the body needs to soften and slow down rather than be worked intensively.

Available at all five locations

Elemis Hot Mineral Body Boost Massage

60 / 90 min GHS 340 / 440 Book Now

A mineral-rich full-body massage using light to medium pressure to ease muscular discomfort, stimulate circulation and condition the skin. More energising in quality than a standard relaxation massage.

Available at all five locations

Elemis Peaceful Pregnancy Massage

75 min GHS 400 Book Now

Adapted specifically for pregnancy, using safe positioning and gentle pressure to relieve tension through the back, shoulders, hips, hands and feet. Available from the second trimester onward. Not suitable for clients with recent operations, undiagnosed swelling or chronic back conditions.

Available at all five locations

Head, Back and Neck Massage

30 min GHS 300 Book Now

Focused massage for the areas that accumulate the most tension: upper back, neck, shoulders and scalp. Effective for tightness caused by desk work, stress or poor posture.

Available at all five locations

Indian Head Massage

30 min GHS 220 Book Now

An Ayurvedic technique using specific pressure and massage movements across the head, neck and shoulders to release tension and promote calm. Performed seated, without oils.

Available at all five locations

Reflexology

30 / 60 min GHS 220 / 320 Book Now

A pressure-point therapy applied to the feet, based on the principle that specific zones on the sole correspond to different areas of the body. The 60-minute format allows for more thorough coverage and deeper work.

Available at all five locations

Foot Massage

30 min GHS 140 Book Now

Targeted pressure to relieve tension, improve circulation and bring a noticeable sense of ease to the lower legs and feet.

Available at all five locations
