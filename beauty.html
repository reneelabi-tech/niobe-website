Eyes

Lash extensions, tinting, refills and removal. Brow lamination, staining, tinting and threading. Quick, precise services that make a visible difference. Every treatment is carried out with attention to detail and an understanding of what works for your individual features.

Brows

Brow shaping, tinting, staining and lamination. Each treatment is adapted to your brow shape, hair density and desired result. A patch test may be required before tinting treatments for first-time clients.

Eyebrow Tinting

20 min GHS 100 Book Now

Semi-permanent dye applied to enhance, define and deepen brow colour.

Available at all five locations

Eyebrow Stain

15 min GHS 200 Book Now

Stains both skin and hair for a fuller, more defined brow shape. A popular alternative to traditional tinting and henna.

Available at East Legon · Cantonments · African Regent Hotel

Eyebrow Lamination

45 min GHS 300 Book Now

Sets hairs in place for a fuller, more groomed appearance. Particularly effective for thinning or unruly brows. No needles or colour involved.

Available at East Legon · Cantonments · Community 18 · Alisa Hotel Tema

Lashes

From a quick tint to a full set of semi-permanent extensions. Our lash technicians apply extensions one-to-one with your natural lashes, adapting curl, length and volume to your preference.

Eyelash Tinting

20 min GHS 100 Book Now

Semi-permanent dye for a darker, fuller lash look without mascara. Lasts approximately four weeks.

Available at all five locations

Eyelash and Eyebrow Tinting

30 min GHS 180 Book Now

Combined lash and brow tinting in a single appointment for a defined, polished finish.

Available at all five locations

Individual Eyelash

30 min GHS 200 Book Now

Individual lash clusters applied to enhance length and volume for a natural-looking result.

Available at all five locations

Semi-Permanent Individual Eyelash Extensions

120 min GHS 550 Book Now

Individual extensions bonded one-to-one with natural lashes. Creates a longer, fuller lash line lasting several weeks with proper care.

Available at East Legon · Community 18 · African Regent Hotel

Eyelash Refill

60 min GHS 400 Book Now

Maintenance for existing extensions — replaces naturally shed lashes and restores fullness.

Eyelash Removal

Professional removal using a gentle dissolving solution to protect natural lashes.

Available at all five locations

Threading

Precise hair removal using twisted cotton thread. Threading is suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin that may not tolerate waxing.

Full Face Threading

45 min GHS 250 Book Now

Precise hair removal using twisted cotton thread. Covers the full face including brows, upper lip, chin and cheeks.

Available at all five locations
