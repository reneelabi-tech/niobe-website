Policy

How Niobe Spa collects, uses and protects your personal information.

Last updated: April 2025

Niobe Spa (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used and stored. By booking with us or completing a consultation form, you agree to this policy.

1. Information we collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact details — name, phone number, WhatsApp number, email address, home address or town.
  • Booking information — preferred branch, appointment date, services selected.
  • Health information — disclosed via consultation forms prior to treatment. This includes medical conditions, medications, allergies, and other health factors relevant to safe service delivery.
  • Payment information — processed securely through our payment providers. We do not store full card details.
  • Marketing preferences — if you subscribe to our newsletter or promotions.

2. How we use your information

  • To manage your bookings and appointments.
  • To provide safe, personalised treatments by informing your therapist of relevant health information.
  • To maintain your client records for future visits.
  • To contact you about your appointment, including confirmations and reminders.
  • To send you offers and updates, if you have opted in.
  • To comply with our legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Health information

Health data collected via our consultation forms is classified as sensitive personal data. It is used solely to ensure that treatments are carried out safely and appropriately for each client. This information is accessible only to relevant Niobe Spa therapists and management, and is retained in your client record.

4. How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your data with:

  • Booking and scheduling software used to manage appointments (data is processed securely on our behalf).
  • Payment processors to handle transactions securely.
  • Regulatory authorities if required by law.

5. How long we keep your information

We retain your client records for as long as you remain an active client, and for a reasonable period thereafter in case you return. Consultation form data is kept in line with our record-keeping obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information.
  • Ask us to delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@niobebeauty.com.

7. Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss or misuse. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for all data in transit.

8. Cookies

Our website uses session storage to maintain your progress through multi-step forms (e.g. gift card selection). This data is not transmitted to any server and is cleared when you close your browser tab. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics at this time.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The most recent version will always be available at this page. Continued use of our services after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
