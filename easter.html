Easter Packages

Available across our locations in Accra and Tema. A 100% non-refundable deposit is required to confirm your booking.

First Light

65 min GHS 620 Book Now

A gentle recalibration. The Elemis Skin Booster Facial lifts away dullness and congestion in thirty careful minutes, revealing a clearer, brighter canvas beneath. From there, tension held in the scalp and temples is slowly unwound through Indian head massage, while a dedicated arm massage eases tightness you may not have realised you were carrying. Small in duration, significant in effect.

30-min Elemis Skin Booster Facial · Indian Head Massage · Arm Massage

The Soft Reawakening

120 min GHS 650 Book Now

Some days call for polish, and this package is perfect for them. A full hour of Elemis deep tissue massage works through the body, releasing held tension and restoring ease. The Sole Delight Pedicure with a Spa Manicure follows, attending to hands and feet with the kind of precision that makes everything feel considered. You leave looking exactly as put together as you feel.

60-min Elemis Freestyle Deep Tissue Massage · Sole Delight Pedicure · Spa Manicure

In Full Bloom

150 min GHS 970 Book Now

Here, body and face receive equal attention. An hour of Garden of England Rose Massage across the head, back and neck dissolves deep-seated tension and restores a sense of physical ease you may have forgotten was possible. The Elemis Superfood Radiance Facial then nourishes the skin over thirty minutes, building a visible glow from within. A pedicure and soothing foot massage close the session, grounding you entirely. This is what it feels like when nothing has been overlooked.

60-min Garden of England Rose Massage · 30-min Elemis Superfood Radiance Facial · Pedicure & Foot Massage

Gentle Return

120 min GHS 730 Book Now

The Elemis Lime and Ginger Salt Scrub opens the ritual with warmth and texture, buffing the skin smooth while the citrus and spice notes wake the senses. Steam follows, opening the pores and softening the body into stillness. Then, the centrepiece — a sixty-minute Elemis Hot Mineral Body Boost Massage uses heated minerals to reach deep into fatigued muscles, coaxing out every held knot and ache. For anyone craving total physical release, this is it.

Steam · 30-min Elemis Lime & Ginger Salt Scrub · 60-min Elemis Hot Mineral Body Boost Massage
