Thermal Facilities

Steam, Sauna
& Jacuzzi

Thermal sessions are twenty minutes and work well before a massage or body treatment to warm and prepare the muscles. Each can be booked as a standalone or added to any treatment at a reduced rate. Steam and sauna are available at East Legon, Cantonments, Community 18 and Alisa Hotel. The jacuzzi is available at East Legon, Cantonments and Community 18.

Steam, Sauna & Jacuzzi

Thermal sessions prepare the body for treatment or provide a standalone moment of deep relaxation. All sessions are twenty minutes. When added to another treatment, the add-on rate applies. Swimwear is required for the jacuzzi.

Steam

20 min GHS 250 / GHS 200 add-on Book Now

Wet heat opens the pores and softens the skin — a useful preparation for body treatments, scrubs or massage. Particularly effective before a wrap or deep tissue massage.

Available at East Legon · Cantonments · Community 18 · Alisa Hotel Tema

Sauna

20 min GHS 250 / GHS 200 add-on Book Now

Dry heat supports circulation and helps ease muscle tension. A good complement to any body treatment, particularly massage or body wraps.

Available at East Legon · Cantonments · Community 18 · Alisa Hotel Tema

Jacuzzi

20 min GHS 250 / GHS 200 add-on Book Now

Warm, pressurised water loosens tight muscles and helps reduce joint discomfort. Swimwear is required. Available at East Legon, Cantonments and Community 18.

Available at East Legon · Cantonments · Community 18

Please inform us of any health conditions, including cardiovascular conditions, high blood pressure or pregnancy, before using thermal facilities. Steam and sauna are not recommended immediately after certain treatments or for clients with specific medical conditions.
