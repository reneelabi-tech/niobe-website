Booking

How do I make a booking?

You can book directly through the online booking system at each location. Visit our booking page and select your preferred location to be taken to that branch's booking portal. You can also reach us via WhatsApp on 0561 11 00 00 or through the contact form.

Is a deposit required?

Yes. A 100% deposit is required at the time of booking. All deposits are non-refundable.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Because deposits are 100% non-refundable, we are unable to offer refunds for cancellations. If you need to reschedule, please contact us as early as possible — we will do our best to move your appointment where availability allows. Please note that rescheduling is subject to availability and is not guaranteed.

Is there a group discount?

Yes. A 5% discount applies when two or more guests book together for the same session. This applies to spa packages, facials and body wraps. Please mention the group booking when reserving your appointments.

How do I pay?

We accept cash, bank transfer, Visa, MTN Mobile Money and Telecel. For bank transfers: Zenith Bank, East Legon branch — Account Name: Niobe Salon and Spa Ltd — Account No: 6011304206. Mobile Money: MTN 0242 62 62 00 or Telecel 0204 62 62 00 (Name: Niobe Spa / Kofi Labi).

Your visit

When should I arrive?

Please arrive at least 10–15 minutes before your appointment. This gives you time to change, complete any consultation form and settle before your treatment begins. Late arrivals may result in a reduced treatment time.

What should I wear?

We provide robes and towels for all spa treatments. For massage and body treatments, you will be appropriately covered at all times. Comfortable, easy-to-remove clothing is recommended for your arrival.

Do I need to bring anything?

Nothing specific is required. If you are coming for a long session or full-day package, you may wish to bring a change of clothes. Swimwear is required for the jacuzzi. We provide all products, towels and robes.

Are there any health conditions I should mention?

Yes. Please inform us of any medical conditions, allergies, recent surgeries, pregnancy or skin sensitivities before your appointment. This allows your therapist to adapt the treatment safely. Certain treatments — such as pregnancy massage — have specific eligibility requirements (available from the second trimester onward, not suitable for recent operations or undiagnosed swelling).

Treatments

Are your therapists trained to use Elemis products?

Yes. Niobe is a certified Elemis partner. All therapists who perform Elemis treatments are professionally trained in the brand's protocols, including BIOTEC technology facials, body treatments and massage techniques.

Can I request a specific therapist?

Yes, you can request a specific therapist when booking, subject to availability. Please mention your preference via WhatsApp or through the contact form and we will do our best to accommodate the request.

Are all treatments available at every location?

Most treatments are available at all five locations, but some services vary by branch. Body wraps and scrubs are not available at African Regent Hotel. The jacuzzi is available at East Legon, Cantonments and Community 18. Steam and sauna are not available at African Regent Hotel. Acrylics are available at East Legon and Community 18 only. The full treatment menu shows availability for each service.

Do your packages include refreshments?

Yes. Every spa package includes refreshments as part of the experience.

Do I need to complete a consultation form?

Yes. A consultation form is required before your first treatment and whenever your health details change. After you book, a link to your form will be included in your booking confirmation email — please complete it in advance of your appointment. Walk-in clients will be asked to complete the form on arrival before their treatment begins. The form takes around five minutes and helps us ensure every treatment is safe and appropriate for you.

Locations

Where are your locations?

We have five locations: East Legon (Accra), Cantonments (Accra), African Regent Hotel (Accra), Community 18 (Tema) and Alisa Hotel (Tema). Each location has its own online booking portal — visit our booking page to select your preferred branch.

Can I book at one location and pay at another?

No — bookings and deposits are location-specific. Please book directly for the branch you intend to visit.
